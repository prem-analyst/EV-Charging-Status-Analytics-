# ⚡ EV Charging Station Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Data Analytics project focused on EV (Electric Vehicle) Charging Station infrastructure performance, revenue optimization, and user behavior trends. This interactive dual-page dashboard was built using **Power BI** to provide actionable business insights for charging network operators.

---

## 🚀 Key Business Insights (Pune Region)

### 📈 1. Revenue & Demand Trends
* **Peak Performance:** Revenue saw a steady upward trajectory throughout the year, peaking significantly in **November (382K INR)**, followed closely by **September (371K INR)**.
* **The March Slump:** A sharp drop in revenue was observed in **March (273K INR)**, indicating a seasonal dip or potential system maintenance window that needs operational review.

### 📍 2. Geospatial & Station Load Analysis
* **Top Revenue Generators:** **Hinjewadi** and **Kothrud** emerge as the highest-performing charging hubs, driving maximum grid utilization and customer traffic due to their high IT/commercial density.
* **Growth Opportunities:** **Kharadi** and **Shivajinagar** represent high-potential areas currently displaying lower comparative revenues, signaling a need for targeted marketing or promotional pricing strategies.

### 🔌 3. Charger & Vehicle Dynamics
* **High-Speed Dominance:** **DC Ultra-Fast Chargers** heavily dominate revenue shares, highlighting that consumers strongly prioritize minimizing charging duration over cost.
* **Target Audience:** **4-Wheeler SUVs and Sedans** contribute the highest share of energy consumption, reflecting the primary consumer base utilizing public fast-charging infrastructure.
